Who I Am

About Me

I am a computational biologist and bioinformatics scientist with 10+ years of research experience, including five years of postdoctoral research at University of California, San Francisco (UCSF's) at Liao Lab, Department of Dermatology.

My research sits at the intersection of single-cell multi-omics, spatial transcriptomics, genome-wide association studies, and machine learning as well as Computational chemoinformatics and Drug designing applied to complex human inflammatory, cancers and autoimmune diseases — including Psoriasis, Atopic Dermatitis, Psoriatic Arthritis, Spondyloarthritis, RA, and Hidradenitis Suppurativa.

I develop scalable analytical pipelines, discover clinically actionable biomarkers, and translate high-dimensional omics data into mechanistic insights that inform precision medicine and therapeutic development.

I have collaborated with pharmaceutical partners including Amgen, Johnson & Johnson (Janssen), LEO Pharma, and TrexBio, and contributed to multiple NIH-funded research programs.

Career History

Work Experience

🏛️
June 2021 – Present
Postdoctoral Research Fellow
University of California, San Francisco (UCSF) — Department of Dermatology, Liao Lab
  • Single-Cell Multi-Omics: Designed end-to-end pipelines for scRNA-seq, CITE-seq (198 surface proteins), scATAC-seq, and Xenium Spatial Transcriptomics across >200 samples in Psoriasis, Atopic Dermatitis, PsA, and Hidradenitis Suppurativa.
  • Spatial Biology: Led Xenium In Situ studies with 5,100-gene panel (4 samples/slide); identified MPZ+S100B+ Schwann cells as novel neuroimmune hubs in AD skin — under review at JACI 2025.
  • GWAS & PRS: Completed multi-ethnic GWAS (>20,000 samples); built validated polygenic risk score engines with Leave-One-Out sensitivity analysis.
  • ML & Pharmacogenomics: Applied Random Forest, XGBoost, CNN to Janssen Phase III clinical trial data to predict biologic treatment response; CITE-seq ML classifier ~87% accuracy (Nature Comm, 2025).
  • AI Engineering: Directed development of computer vision web tool for automated real-time PASI score tracking from sequential patient images.
  • Collaboration: Contributed preliminary data to NIH R01 grants; partnered with Amgen, J&J, LEO Pharma, and TrexBio; mentored 5+ undergraduate and MD fellows.
